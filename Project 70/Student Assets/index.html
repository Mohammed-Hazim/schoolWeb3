Campus Life

School Church

Was established in 1978

Students go to the church every Saturday and Sunday after the morning bell to come for church, where two three teachers take turns talking about good behaviour and moral manner. The students are also gaining knowledge about religion and this all helps them become good citizens in the future.



school library

Was built in 1980

The school library is an interactive place for the students of Benchmarks International School to enrich their knowledge and help them grow their interest in books. There are library classes on Tuesday, Wednesday and Friday.



school auditorium

Was built in 1982

The school auditorium is where all the school assemblies, Arts meets, funtions, etc, are held. This is a very memorable place for the students because of the various functions that have happened and are going to. The school auditorium is one of the top 5 biggest school auditoriums in India with more than 5000 seats.

School Achievements

Inter School Debate On Environment Pollution
Won first prize in inter school debate on environment poluution

Our school proudly stands first out of 28 schools in the inter school debate that was held at Mumbai on 20th January. The debate champion was Isha Azmin of 11th grade. Our students have shown everyone the great intelect of our school and our students through a debate about environment pollution.

Inter School Football Copetition
Came second in inter school football competition

Our mighty school is also the holder of second prize in the inter school football competition also held in Mumbai on 27th January. Our school team with their coach, Mr. Abdul Razak, came second amongst 42 schools with great opposition. Still, they blasted through like Messi and Ronaldo and came second.

Inter School Science Project Competition
Won second prize in inter school Science Project competition

Benchmarks International School is again in the top ranks after winning yet another second place, this time in the wonderous subject of science. This time, the competition was at Delhi on 13th February. Our students' great minds, inspired by great scientists ike Dr. APJ Abdul Kalam and CV Raman, made a self-sustaining ecosytem with its own weather system. We are very proud of what our team of students have presented to the judges and their determination has shown benefit of being in the top ranks among 28 schools.
